# Subtractor



A subtractor is a circuit which gives the difference between two input data along with the borrow. A half subtractor only performs subtraction on two 1-bit data and gives the output as difference and borrow. A full subtractor considers the previous borrow hence takes total three inputs as two data bits like A and B along with a borrow input.



A half subtractor circuit is nothing but a adder circuit with one extra inverter present before the input of AND gate. A full subtractor can be implemented by using two half subtractor along with a OR gate.
